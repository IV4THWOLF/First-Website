Artist

Devon T. Lowery

was born and raised in Baltimore, Maryland. He began studying the arts on his own in 2005, creating digital artwork and from that point he found his love for the visual arts. He's currently studying  at Morgan State University and expecting to graduate with his B.A. in December of 2014. He specializes in Automotive Photography and his love for the visual art comes from the idea he has of  "making your ideas a reality." "You've got to be maneuverable with your mind. Let your ideas materialize." It's this same statement that continues to motivate Devon as an artist.
